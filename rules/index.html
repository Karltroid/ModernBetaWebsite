Minecraft Rules Discord Rules

  1. Consent Is Key


    Do not take or break things without permission from the player its owned by from via voice, text, or a sign they placed. If no explicit or obvious permission is given, assume you can't use/take/modify it. Every action you do is logged and we will find out.

  2. No Hacking


    Using any form of software/tool to give you an advantage in the game. Examples include XRay, fly hacks, etc. We DO allow tools like fullbright, shaders, mini maps that don't reveal player locations, and performance mods

  3. No Exploits


    Do not use exploits to gain an advantage in the game. If you discover an exploit please report it in our discord. We do not allow duplication exploits especially EXCEPT for sand/gravel exploit using pistons (cause I'd rather that than dug up beaches/deserts)
  4. All other rules in Discord Rules apply here too <3

  1. Use Common Sense


    Think before you act.

  2. No Spamming


    Spamming or flooding the discord chats is not allowed.

  3. Using Direct/Indirect Threats


    Threatening other discord users including but not limited to DDoS, Dox, Death and anything that we deem malicious.

  4. Inappropriate Content/Links


    Any content or links that are deemed inappropriate are disallowed.

  5. Support/Ticket Trolling


    If you intentionally mess around or waste the time of our staff members, you will be punished.

  6. Inappropriate Language


    Swearing is allowed, however, no non-contextual(Random) or overuse of inappropriate language, directed towards someone or not. Any racial language is NOT allowed.

  7. No Drama


    Do not start any squabbles or drama in this discord, if you have an issue with someone then ⁠create-ticket

  8. Advertising


    Within this discord you may only Advertise your videos and live streams that involve Modern Beta in⁠self-promo, anything else will be considered a breach of the rule and you will be punished. Do not repeatedly post allowed advertisements either.

  9. Harassment of Users/Players


    Harassing users or players using our services is not allowed, this includes asking very personal questions.

  10. Poaching Other Users/Players


    Trying to take players/users from our services is not allowed, an example of this is using the ModernBeta discord to Private Message other members links to your platforms. Discord links sent in the discord are automatically removed and will kick you if spammed.
