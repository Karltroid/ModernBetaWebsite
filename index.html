What is Modern Beta?

Modern Beta is a recreation of the infamous Minecraft version from 2011, beta 1.7.3, in modern day Minecraft (1.20.4) utilizing resource pack and server side magic. Beta 1.7.3 is widley recognized as the "Golden Age" version of the game due to its simplicity, charm, and lack of "forced goals," so the only goal is the ones you make with you and your friends.
